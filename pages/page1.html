The Importance of a Custom Website in the Digital Age

  • Hanna
  • Oct 26, 2021

With the rise of “drag and drop” website builders, all of which tend to look rather similar to one another, the value of a dedicated website developer cannot be understated. Although streamlined sites may be more approachable to the average business person, their use of templates means that no matter how much you try you’ll always find a site that looks strikingly similar to another. Minimal features and not much room for change or growth leaves many of these prefabricated sites looking a bit bland and repetitive.

That’s why using our services is the choice to make. At SkryTech we are dedicated to ensuring smooth, professional consultation and execution of your brand identity onto the web.

In a time where more people than ever are utilizing the internet to research and purchase goods and services, a personalized website serves as the “face” of your company.

Below are four simple but important reasons to invest in your company by investing in a custom website:

  • Identity
  • Discoverability
  • Flexibility
  • Accessibility

    • Identity: your individuality, translated to the web

    In a time where more people than ever are utilizing the internet to research and purchase goods and services, a personalized website serves as the “face” of your company. It’s what many potential customers will see first. Custom design and functionality offers first time visitors — as well as those who are returning — a familiar face.

    Through our services, your website will excellently display your value to any and all potential clients alongside what you believe, and who your company is.

    Beyond that, picking and choosing your own color palette, font, and more lets you tell your business’ story outside of the written word. The design that you curate is just as important as any “About Us” page.

    Discoverability: simplifying the search

    Catching the eye of any potential clientele online starts with effective, strategic search engine optimization (SEO).

    When you use a search engine, it combs through a massive number of websites to find relevant results. The results closest to the top are there for multiple reasons, but well executed SEO is a primary one. While the design and layout of your site is important in securing transactions, precise SEO makes sure search engines prioritize your website and present it above the competition.

    By thoughtfully crafting on-page and off-page keywords, as well as content, more traffic is driven to your site. The more traffic driven to your site, the more conversions. Balancing effective SEO and design — that is, not focused too intently on either but instead on both equally — promises that your website will retain its identity without sacrificing discoverability.

    Flexibility: ability to grow and adapt

    You know your business best, which means you know when it’s time for a fresh coat of paint. Choosing to closely work with a web designer who’s already familiar with your business makes this process even easier.

    If, at any point, you see the need to switch things up or add onto what you’ve already commissioned, it’s as simple as sending a short email.

    Availability: accessible to your clients, 24/7:

    Unfortunately human beings can’t be everywhere at once. There’s errands to run, phone calls to make, meetings to be held, and many more everyday hurdles we have to contend with. And, sometimes, you just can’t be available for whatever reason — it happens to us all.

    That’s where your website comes in.

    When life gets in the way, your website remains up and running. It’s reliably able to deliver any and all information that a client may need, making things like e-mail forms or menus accessible at all times. While you’re occupied elsewhere, your custom-built site will take care of the busy work.

    There are many reasons to use custom web design services, and these are just a few. Consult with us to find out more.
